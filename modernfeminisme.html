What is Modern Feminism?

Modern feminism, also called fourth-wave feminism, focuses on *equality, inclusion, and digital activism*. Unlike earlier waves, it emphasizes intersectionality — recognizing how race, class, sexuality, and culture influence the experience of women.

Key Goals

  • Ending gender-based violence and harassment.
  • Promoting equal pay and economic opportunities.
  • Supporting reproductive rights and healthcare access.
  • Encouraging body positivity and self-expression.
  • Raising awareness through social media campaigns.
"Feminism is for everyone." – bell hooks

Modern Feminism Movements

Social media has played a crucial role in spreading awareness. Movements like #MeToo and #TimesUp have highlighted harassment, gender inequality, and workplace discrimination globally.
