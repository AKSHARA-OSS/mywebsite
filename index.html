What is Feminism?

Feminism is a social and political movement that fights against discrimination, stereotypes, and unfair treatment of women. It aims to ensure that women have the same freedom, power, and choices as men in society. Feminists work to remove gender-based inequalities and promote respect and dignity for everyone, regardless of gender.

Why It Matters

Feminism helps create a fair society where everyone has the same chance to succeed, regardless of gender. It supports education, equality at work, and protection from discrimination.
